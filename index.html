Our Story

Keystone Surveys specialises in offering premium surveying and engineering solutions to a diverse clientele. With a strong focus on accuracy and client satisfaction, we bring expertise and commitment to every project, big or small.

With 20+ years of engineering surveying experience, We bring a wealth of knowledge across disciplines. Our expertise encompasses everything from land surveying to construction management, ensuring precise and efficient execution. Our expertise includes installing control networks, traversing, setting out, and performing precise engineering surveys. Additionally, we specialise in machine control, laser scanning, and UAV surveying and inspections, ensuring the highest level of accuracy and efficiency.

We are dedicated to delivering high-quality results of accuracy efficiently.

Trust us to deliver innovative solutions tailored to meet your needs. Let's work together to achieve your project goals.
